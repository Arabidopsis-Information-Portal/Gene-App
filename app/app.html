This app displays tables containing data, about a certain gene.

Instructions:

Enter a locus ID in the input box. An example is shown below the search box. Once search is clicked the tables in the tabs will be loaded with data. Click on any of the tabs to view that table. To search for another gene, click clear and search again.

The Locus ID follows the format: ATXgYYYYY. The AT refers to Arabidopsis thaliana, the X number corresponds to the chromosome number, the g refers to gene, and the 5 following Ys correspond to the five-digit code, numbered from top to bottom of the chromosome.
